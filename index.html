This game is a simple and entertaining game that allows you to learn about Vermont's geography, and learn to use geographic coordinates.

To play, just click "Start a Game." The Map will move to a random point in the center of Vermont. An icon with Ethan Allen, a historic Vermont political figure, will appear at the center of the map. To identify the location of the icon, use the nine (9) navigation buttons to move a small distance in each direction. Points will be deducted each time you use the navigation buttons, except for the home button in the center, which takes you back to the starting coordinates. You can also zoom in and out with the buttons on the magnifying glass, but you will lose 150 points each time you zoom out.

When you think you may know the County, click "Guess the Spot." Select the correct County to win. If you click the wrong county, you will lose 150 points.
